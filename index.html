The Journey...

Red has grown through the community by starting off as a Moderator on PopularMMOs World, leading to him taking on the role of a script writer and script creator for popular quest-lines featured on both PokeFind and PopularMMOs World! His strive for excellence exceeded the minds of those whom ran the services he used leading to him taking on numerous roles including being a Builder and Quality Assurance Member for the PopularMMOs World Server!

His experience in building and staffing lead to him applying for the role as Builder for the well-known map creator who goes by the name of Lubcubs Gaming where he created maps for PopularMMOs and BeckBroJack! Now he creates for Lubcubs along with a group of people from the 100 Media company compound whom build for the well-known creators such as MooseCraft and UnspeakableGaming and many more!
